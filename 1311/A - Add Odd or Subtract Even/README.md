Add Odd or Subtract Even</div><div class="time-limit"><div class="property-title">time limit per test</div>2 seconds</div><div class="memory-limit"><div class="property-title">memory limit per test</div>256 megabytes</div><div class="input-file input-standard"><div class="property-title">input</div>standard input</div><div class="output-file output-standard"><div class="property-title">output</div>standard output</div></div><div><p>You are given two positive integers $$$a$$$ and $$$b$$$.</p><p>In one move, you can <span class="tex-font-style-bf">change</span> $$$a$$$ in the following way:</p><ul> <li> Choose any positive <span class="tex-font-style-bf">odd</span> integer $$$x$$$ ($$$x  \gt  0$$$) and replace $$$a$$$ with $$$a+x$$$; </li><li> choose any positive <span class="tex-font-style-bf">even</span> integer $$$y$$$ ($$$y  \gt  0$$$) and replace $$$a$$$ with $$$a-y$$$. </li></ul><p>You can perform as many such operations as you want. You can choose the same numbers $$$x$$$ and $$$y$$$ in different moves.</p><p>Your task is to find the minimum number of moves required to obtain $$$b$$$ from $$$a$$$.
